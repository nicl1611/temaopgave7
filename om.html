About Sticker Mania

We are four enthusiasts who are really happy with stickers in all shapes and sizes.

It started as a hobby but has quickly become a bigger passion as we found out how big a community there actually is within stickers.

These are our own collections, which we share here on the site.

We have bought and paid for all the stickers ourselves, nothing is sponsored (unless otherwise stated) and we have taken all the pictures and written all the texts ourselves.

The goal of our site is to create more awareness around handmade stickers and the small shops with self-employed people around the world. Especially during the Covid-19 Pandemic it is important to support locally.

We hope you find some artists on this page that you will support.
